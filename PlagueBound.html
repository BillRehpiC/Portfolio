About this game

“A mutant hero battles virus-altered foes in alternate history sidescroller” Plaguebound is a sidescroller and 2D platformer game set in an alternate timeline where a biology alternating virus attacked the world again. We follow our main protagonist, a mutated human that, mysteriously, managed to gain the mutation's powers while also maintaining his human cognitive functions, making him a powerful being that could take the fight to the entities that emerged from the mutations.
